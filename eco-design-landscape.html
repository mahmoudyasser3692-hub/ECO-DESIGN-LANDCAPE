How It Works

Our Design Process

A thoughtful, collaborative journey from first conversation to flourishing landscape.

01

Discovery & Consultation

We listen to your vision, assess your site, understand your lifestyle, and identify ecological opportunities unique to your property.

02

Concept Design

Our team develops multiple concept directions, exploring spatial layouts, plant palettes, and sustainable feature options.

03

Detailed Planning

We refine the chosen concept into a detailed design with planting plans, irrigation layouts, and construction documents.

04

Installation & Care

Expert installation by our team, followed by a care plan and guidance to ensure your landscape flourishes for years to come.
